Why Sustify Warum Sustify

We believe in a bottom-up approach to ensure compliance beyond auditing. We help to understand, we increase awareness, and we prepare the grounds for change. We are digital because it’s a great way to teach and track how well something is being learnt. Wir glauben an einen Bottom-up-Ansatz, um die Einhaltung über die Prüfung hinaus sicherzustellen. Wir helfen zu verstehen, wir sensibilisieren und bereiten die Gründe für Veränderungen vor. Wir sind digital, weil es eine großartige Möglichkeit ist, zu lehren und zu verfolgen, wie gut etwas gelernt wird.

Micro-learning Mikrolernen

Content is limited to be relevant to day-to-day factory life, provided in small “bites”. So Micro-learning is a skill based learning and education which deals with relatively small learning units. ___

Personalized Personalisiert

Content adapts accordingly to individual learning progress, based on an intelligent algorithm respecting learning methodology of spacing and repetition. ___

Inclusive Inklusive

Login with face recognition, multilinguality and audio function make sure, that the entire workforce can be reached and educated. ___

Interactive Interaktiv

Different game formats ensure, that learning is done in different ways, requiring different types of interaction, which increases retention. ___

Customizable Anpassbar

Due to the Content Management System, content can easily be adapted to special requirements. ___

Measurable Messbar

The KPI Dashboard displays demographic data, monitors the attendance of users and analyzes results (anonymized averages). ___
